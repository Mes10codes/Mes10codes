F.A.Q

How many Trippie Headz will there be?

There will be 4,444 Trippie Headz minted for this collection made in collaboration with @Trippie Redd and designed by @YaLocalOffgod

How much will it cost to mint a Trippie Head?

A Trippie Head will available to be minted at a price of .099 ETH.

What is the maximum amount of Trippie Headz I can mint?

A limit of three Trippie Headz per transaction has been established.

How do I mint a Trippie Head, and how will the release be fair?

You will only be able to mint a Trippie Head directly through our website (https://mint.trippieheadz.io/). There will be a small, simple challenge on the website before purchase to add fairness.

What chain is the Trippie Headz collection hosted on?

Trippie Headz is an Ethereum-based NFT collection.

Are the Trippie Headz generated, or hand-drawn?

The vast majority (4444) of Trippie Headz will be generated with unique traits/rarities. There will also be 9 hand-drawn unique 1/1 Trippie Headz.

When is the release date?

The release date is on Wednesday, November 4th, 2021 @3 PM EST.

I still have a question not answered in this FAQ, how can I find out?

Head over to the support-ticket channel on Discord. We will make sure to address any additional questions you may have as soon as possible!
